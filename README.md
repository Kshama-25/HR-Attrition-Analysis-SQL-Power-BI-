# HR-Attrition-Analysis-SQL-Power-BI-
HR Attrition Analysis using SQL &amp; Power BI to identify key factors affecting employee turnover. Explores impact of overtime, satisfaction, work-life balance, and experience. Insights help improve retention, optimize HR strategies, and support data-driven workforce decisions.
This project focuses on analyzing an HR employee dataset to understand the key factors influencing employee attrition and overall workforce behavior. The main objective of this analysis is to identify patterns and trends that can help organizations improve employee retention and make informed, data-driven HR decisions.
The dataset contains various employee-related attributes such as age, gender, education, job role, work experience, satisfaction levels, work-life balance, and attrition status. Using SQL, the data was cleaned, transformed, and analyzed to extract meaningful insights. Multiple queries were written to calculate the overall attrition rate, group employees based on different attributes, and evaluate relationships between critical factors such as overtime, job satisfaction, and experience.
The analysis revealed several important insights. Employees who work overtime were found to have a significantly higher likelihood of leaving the organization, indicating that workload is a major driver of attrition. Additionally, lower levels of job satisfaction, environment satisfaction, and relationship satisfaction were strongly linked to increased employee turnover. Work-life balance also played a crucial role, as employees with better balance tended to stay longer within the company.
Furthermore, experience and career growth opportunities were identified as key factors impacting retention. Employees in the early stages of their careers showed higher attrition, while more experienced employees demonstrated greater stability. A lack of timely promotions and career progression was also observed as a potential reason for decreased motivation and increased attrition risk.
Power BI was used to create interactive dashboards and visualizations, enabling better understanding of workforce trends. This project demonstrates how SQL and Power BI can be effectively combined to transform raw HR data into actionable insights, helping organizations build a more stable, productive, and engaged workforce.
